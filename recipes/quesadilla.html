Quesadilla Recipe

Description

Today we will learn how to make a delicious quesadilla in just a few easy steps. This is a quick and easy meal to make and with enough chicken made, you can eat on this for days!! Get ready to follow along and hope you enjoy this simple recipe!!

To see a video tutorial on making a quesadilla,Click Here

Ingredients

  • Chicken (2-3 lbs)
  • Tapatio Slow Cooker Marinade
  • Cheese
  • Tortillas
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Add chicken and Tapatio Slow Cooker Marinade into a crock pot.
  2. Cook chicken in a crockpot on low for 4-7 hours.
  3. Once chicken is finished, shred inside the crockpot using forks.
  4. After you have shredded the chicken, grab your tortillas and butter.
  5. Butter up your tortilla, and place in a pan on medium heat.
  6. Add your cheese and chicken, then cook till desired crispiness.
  7. Serve with Salsa and Pico de Gallo or whatever else you like to put on a quesadilla.
